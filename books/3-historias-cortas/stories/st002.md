## Story 2: The Brave Cub
Lily woke up to find her cave full of warm sunshine. She was curious about what had happened and looked out through the big window. There in the distance she saw a small boat floating away on the calm water.

Lily the Bear watched carefully as the boat moved away from her cave. She was curious and wanted to know more about what she saw.

Lily the Bear felt Patient as she watched the boat drift away. She wished very much to know more about what happened. Her heart ached for the people on the small boat. She thought and thought but no idea popped into her head. Suddenly, Lily saw something unusual in her cave - an invisible cloak! It was as if magic had appeared before her eyes. Lily decided she needed this invisible cloak. With it, she could follow the boat and find out what happened to those people.

The Friendly Wizard appeared in a puff of smoke, with a smile on his face. He handed Lily an invisible cloak and said softly, “Use this to see what’s beyond your cave.” Lily hugged the cloak tightly as her heart beat faster. She wondered if she could really do it.

Lily the Bear tried hard. She climbed high up on a rock to see better through her invisible cloak. The sun was hot and made her sweat. Her arms got tired from holding it so long. Lily kept looking, hoping to catch a glimpse of the boat. Finally, she saw faint shapes moving in the distance. She realized she had found something brave within herself - enough courage to try.

The sun was bright and warm. Lily held her invisible cloak tightly as she looked out at the vast land beyond her cave. She had climbed high on a big rock to see better through her new tool.
Lily saw tiny shapes that moved in the distance, like little animals playing by the lake. Her heart felt happy because she could finally see something with her new cloak. The invisible cloak had helped Lily find courage and look outside her cave.

Lily felt a bit sleepy but knew she had made something brave within herself - enough courage to try. She watched the shapes move for a while longer before she went back inside her cave. The sun hung high in the sky, its warm rays making everything feel cozy and safe. Lily closed her eyes, feeling grateful for all she had found that day with her invisible cloak. From his hiding spot near the entrance, the Friendly Wizard waved gently to say goodbye.



Lesson: Courage grows little by little
