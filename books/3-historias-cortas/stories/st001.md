## Story 1: The Determined Penguin
Penguin Ella and her friend Puppy are at the beach. Ella is feeling hopeful today.

Puppy looks at Ella with big eyes and a smile. Ella shows Puppy the map that says go to the castle.

Ella thinks if they follow this map, there might be surprises like pretty flowers or funny animals. She wants to find out what's on the other side of the door.

The door won't open though. It feels stubborn and hard. Ella frowns a little but stays hopeful. "Let's try again," she says with her friendly smile. Puppy nods, ready for another adventure. The castle is just around the corner from here.

The castle is just around the corner from here. Busy Beaver flies into view with a big smile. Ella sees her and waves excitedly. Puppy looks at Busy Beaver curiously. "Hi there! I heard you two have some trouble," says Busy Beaver, showing Ella the Star Necklace. "This necklace has superpowers to help you find what you seek." "I want us to listen carefully now," says Busy Beaver, giving a piece of advice. "Listen with your heart and see where it takes you.

Penguin looks down at her hands. She nods to herself, feeling brave and determined. "Okay," she says softly. Her eyes light up with hope as she listens carefully, trying hard not to let the door’s stubbornness get to her.

The Star Necklace shines brightly in her hand, its simple power guiding Ella through the darkness of doubt. With Puppy watching from a distance, Ella closes her eyes and hears the castle whispering. She feels like it's telling her where to look next.

The door finally opens with a loud creak, its hinges groaning from years of use. Inside, the walls are lined with ancient statues and paintings that seem to come alive as Ella steps inside.

Puppy wags her tail happily as Ella steps out of the castle, her hands still holding the Star Necklace. She feels a warm breeze on her face and sees the beach stretching out before them like a canvas painted with sand and blue sky. "Wow, that was amazing!" Puppy squeals, her eyes wide with wonder. "Did you hear what it said?" Ella smiles warmly at her friend. "I heard it whispering to me. It told me where to look next." The sun is setting in the horizon now, painting the sky with soft oranges and purples.



Lesson: Listening is a superpower
